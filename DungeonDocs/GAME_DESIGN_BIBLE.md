# Game Design Bible: DungeonTrouble

## 1. Vision & Genre
DungeonTrouble is a 2.5D Isometric Action MMORPG emphasizing responsive combat, deep progression, and a vibrant, player-driven economy.

## 2. Target Audience
Mid-core to hardcore MMO players who appreciate skill-based combat, deep crafting systems, and guild-centric social mechanics.

## 3. Gameplay Philosophy
* **Combat**: Action-oriented, hitbox-based, no tab-targeting. Movement and positioning are paramount.
* **Progression**: Meaningful horizontal and vertical progression. No infinite scaling.
* **Classes**: Distinct archetypes with flexible skill trees.
* **Skills**: Synergistic abilities that encourage party play.
* **Crafting & Economy**: Player-driven. Top-tier gear is crafted, not just dropped.
* **NPCs & Bosses**: Complex AI with readable telegraphs and multi-phase mechanics.
* **Loot**: Deterministic elements mixed with RNG. High value placed on crafting materials.

## 4. World & Social
* **Housing**: Instanced and open-world player housing.
* **Guilds**: Guild halls, guild wars, and progression perks.
* **PvP**: Dedicated PvP zones and arenas; balanced separately from PvE.
* **World & Biomes**: Hand-crafted zones with distinct aesthetic identities and environmental hazards.

## 5. Technical Design Requirements
* **Camera**: Fixed 2.5D isometric perspective.
* **Art Direction**: Stylized, readable silhouttes.
* **Animation**: Snappy, responsive, readable wind-ups.
* **UI**: Unobtrusive, highly customizable.
* **Audio**: Thematic scoring and distinct audio cues for critical abilities.
* **Accessibility**: Colorblind modes, remappable inputs, visual audio cues.

## 6. Future Expansion
Expansions will introduce new biomes, classes, and crafting tiers without invalidating previously achieved baseline progression.
